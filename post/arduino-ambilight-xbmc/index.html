Homemade ambilight with Arduino and Kodi

By Guillermo Guerrero | January 15, 2016

The Ambilight system is a gadget for TV to enjoy the TV shows. The system is composed by a concrete RGB LEDs (25 or 50 LEDs) and the LEDs reproduce the border color from TV. This post explains how create a homemade Ambilight system and connect to TV with Kodi.

Components

  • 50 LEDS => 2 x [12mm Diffused Thin Digital RGB LED Pixels (Strand of 25) - WS2801]: Link Ebay
  • Arduino uno: Link Dx
  • Male + Female DC Power Converter Connector Adapters (Optional to connect leds): Link Dx
